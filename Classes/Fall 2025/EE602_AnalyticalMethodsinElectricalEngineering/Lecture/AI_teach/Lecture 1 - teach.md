### Mathematical Examples with Step-by-Step Solutions
#### Example Problem
**Problem Statement**: Solve the following equation for \( x \):
\[
3x + 5 = 20
\]

**Step 1**: Isolate the variable \( x \).
- Subtract 5 from both sides:
\[
3x = 20 - 5
\]
\[
3x = 15
\]

**Step 2**: Divide both sides by 3 to solve for \( x \):
\[
x = \frac{15}{3}
\]
\[
x = 5
\]

**Final Answer**: \( x = 5 \)

### Important Formulas and Their Applications
- **Ohm's Law**: \( V = IR \)
  - Where \( V \) is voltage, \( I \) is current, and \( R \) is resistance. This law is fundamental in circuit analysis.
  
- **Kirchhoff's Laws**:
  - **Kirchhoff's Current Law (KCL)**: The total current entering a junction equals the total current leaving the junction.
  - **Kirchhoff's Voltage Law (KVL)**: The sum of the electrical potential differences (voltage) around any closed network is zero.
